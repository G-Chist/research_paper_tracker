FAQ

How do I add papers?

In order to add a paper, get a link from Arxiv and paste it into the text field on the home page.

How do I remove papers?

In order to remove a paper, click on the x icon next to its title.

How do I access the papers?

The papers are linked to their source page on Arxiv. You can view the paper by clicking the title (highlighted in blue ).
